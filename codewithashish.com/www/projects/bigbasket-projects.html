Projects completed in bigbasket

As a Senior Software Engineer at BigBasket, I have been instrumental in the development and management of key projects that have significantly enhanced the company's operations and user experience.

  1. Vendor Portal: This is a multi-role based portal developed using Angular 2+, Node.JS, Express, MSSQL, Firebase, and OAuth. The portal serves as a platform for BigBasket vendors to list their items and manage their orders effectively. It is designed to be user-friendly and efficient, ensuring seamless interaction between the company and its vendors.
  2. Grocery Management Microservice: This project involves the development of an Angular-based portal to manage the lifecycle and storage of groceries. The technology stack for this project includes Angular 4+, MSSQL, NoSQL, Java, and Node.JS. The portal is designed to ensure the efficient management of grocery items, from procurement to storage, thereby enhancing the overall operational efficiency of the company.
These projects have not only honed my technical skills but also provided me with a deep understanding of the e-commerce industry, particularly in the grocery segment. They have allowed me to contribute significantly to BigBasket's mission of delivering the best quality groceries to its customers.
